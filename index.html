KRAKÓW, AUSCHWITZ-BIRKENAU, WIELICZKA SALT MINE

Krakow, a beautiful and lively city of medieval magic in Poland and one of the most important destination for school history and religious education trips.
A visit to Krakow is incredibly valuable for student to understand a full inhumanity and damage of Holocaust.
Auschwitz-Birkenau Camp - the UNESCO-listed complex where you can learn about terrible past from a knowledgeable guide.
The “Wieliczka” Salt Mine, unique and one of the most valuable monuments of material and spiritual culture in Poland. It is also a world class monument, featuring on the UNESCO’s World Cultural and Natural Heritage List.

HIGHLIGHTS

  • The ornate cloth Hall in the vast Market Square, one of the largest squares in Europe
  • A guided city walking tour of the Old Town, including Wawel Castle
  • The illuminated chamber of Wieliczka Salt Mine - guided tour
  • The lessons of Auschwitz-Birkenau Concentration Camp and Schindler's Factory - guided tour
  • Food tour- a journey into flavours and culture of Polish Cuisine

Write to us and we will send you a detailed plan of your trip with a quote. It will be individually crafted with love, according to your expectations, age group and your budget. Don’t hesitate to contact us.

Zakopane

We are excited to invited you on a trip to Zakopane 'the winter capital' of Poland situated at the foot of Tatra Mountain. A place with unique ambience and stunning views.

HIGHLIGHTS

  • Trip to Morskie Oko, the largest mountain-lake in the High Tatras, lying on the Polish side of the mountains, under the peak of Rysy. The name ‘‘The Eye of the Sea‘‘ is derived from an old legend, according to which the lake was connected to the sea via an underground passage
  • Gubalowka Hill cable car trip with a panoramic, stanning view of the Tatra Mountains, High Beskidy and the Pieniny Mountains
  • Visit the Salt Mine in Wieliczka, a national treasure of Poland, a world class monument, featuring on the UNESCO’s World Cultural and Natural Heritage List
  • Dunajec River Gorge trip in wooden canoes with Goral highlanders
  • Aquapark - Therma Bania Bialka Tatrzanska. Water from thermal pools is characterized by an advantageous level of mineralization, diverse chemical composition, low acidity and bacteriological purity

Write to us and we will send you a detailed plan of your trip with a quote. It will be individually crafted with love, according to your expectations, age group and your budget. Don’t hesitate to contact us.

Brenna

Let us take you to the place situated in the south of Poland, it is like a beautiful rock crystal, found in the mountains of the Silesian Beskid.

HIGHLIGHTS

  • Visit 'The Bread Cottage' - children can try their hands with the quern, bake bread, make real white cheese to the traditional recipe, bake flatbread of Brenna in an oven
  • Galeria Pod Maska Gallery - workshops on ceramics, painting on glass, decoupage and paper-making
  • Bielsko-Biala - trip in gondola lift from Olszówka ( Bielsko-Biała district ) to Szyndzielnia in the Silesian Beskid
  • Visit the Studio of Cartoon Films - Two-hour adventure in which children can see the real scenery of popular cartoon, props, character designs or cartoon drawings. Children and young people learn basic concepts such as movie script, script, animation, chamfering, filming, special effects and sound
  • The Banialuka Puppet Theatre - acting workshops -body awareness, rhythm, theatrical space, the role of working with a partner, concentration. Sharing fun in theatre will allow its participants to launch imagination and more consciously look at the theater, the work of the actor and himself , animation workshops with spectacle - the mysterious world of animation will be introduced through a series of exercises and games culminating in the construction of short theatrical etudes
